About V Dance Academy

   

V Dance Academy is a full-fledged music education provider that empowers students with creativity and knowledge and paves the way for their multi-dimensional growth across various segments of music industry. It organizes events like summer, monsoon & winter sessions followed by stage shows in the fests. In the past, students of Amrapaali Kala Peeth have been selected for many TV shows.

The Institute provides a prestigious platform to its students and helps them in realizing their true potential. Besides that, it also renders financial aid to the deserving students in the form of fee waivers. It also helps placing their students with good organization and they've been doing well in their respective fields.

V Dance believes in the overall development of students. Be it physical, mental or spiritual, the institute ensures a holistic fitness through various forms of dances, aerobics & exercises. Besides Music & Dance, Amrapaali offers a pure and spiritual environment which gets automatically instilled in the heart soul of students.

  Mission

"To be a one -stop-destination for all music & art education needs by providing a friendly and highly professional training environment and infrastructure and to be the obvious choice for all age groups across all segments of the society."

  Vision

"To take the music & art education to the next level by constant innovation and give a new dimension to the stream and become the pioneer in bringing about a paradigm shift in the stream."

Neha Verma

Instructor-Classical & Folk Dance

Ceo of Vdance Academy

Neha born and brought up in Jammu, India. She is a versatile dancer. She has done degree in Kathak from Banaras,U.P. She started her Kathak when she was eight year only, under the guidance of Guru Ram Sankhar Bhatnagar. He was an eminent Kathak dancer in Calcutta city. Neha also learnt Kathak from ravindra bhavan Calcutta.

PROFESSIONAL QUALIFICATION:
  1. M.A. in kathak from prayag sangeet samitee.
  2. senior diploma in vocal.
  3. Western dance Bollywood style from kingdom of dreams.Gurgaon.
ACHIEVEMENTS

Neha has performed several stage shows in Kathak and folk dances in different cities across in India like Orissa, Banaras, Calcutta, shimla, jaipur etc. She awarded in” India theatre Olympiad” for dance and play in Orissa Cuttack. She is Winner in all India classical & folk dance competitions in many cities. Her expressions in dance are attracting the audience. She also Awarded by “cultural development & social welfare department” for best choreography and lead dancer. Wherever she performed everyone appreciate her rhythmic sense or expressions. Neha has danced shiv tandav under the direction of Guru Shankar Bhattacharya. She is also performed Manipuri, oddissi and bharatanatyam dance. She has organized various workshops in Kathak and folk dances. She is not just a good Kathak dancer she has a good command of other Bollywood style and folk dances of India like ghoomar , kaalbeliya, lavani, gidda, dandia, bengli,doongri ,pahari ,karagattam and few tribal dances as well as.
